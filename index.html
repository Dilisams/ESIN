The ESIN Model

One Platform.
Total Water Intelligence.

Live Water Intelligence Map

Real-time state-wide map of every hub, reservoir, and supply zone with stress scoring and shortage alerts.

Community Water Hub System

Decentralized hub management — tank levels, schedules, maintenance, and population analytics in one view.

PPP Supplier Network

Formal onboarding and monitoring of private water operators — tankers, boreholes, reservoirs — with compliance metrics.

Smart Metering System

Digital pay-as-you-use metering with leak detection, consumption forecasting, and affordability safeguards.

AI-driven shortage detection auto-generates priority alerts and routes tankers to critical zones.

Citizen Water Access App

Residents find nearest supply, check availability, report shortages, and request emergency deliveries.
